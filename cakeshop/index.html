Our Story

Hand made with love. Shipped with care. Send a Cake was formed with a simple yet ambitious mission in mind. Creating unique gifting experiences they won't forget centered around cake which is the age old tradition of any celebratory event.

Giving our customers amazing memorable gift options for their loved ones, and being a part of spreading joy, love and the ability to simply brighten up someone's day is icing on the cake! Our small business has been and will always be about people and the experiences.
